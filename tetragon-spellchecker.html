Tetragon Spellchecker

Use this before sending documents to us to ensure consistency of spelling through your document and finding misspellings of difficult names.

Paste the relevant text into the box below. Then select whether you want to spellcheck against a British (-ise or -ize) or American dictionary. You can optionally compare the output from two different dictionaries to determine which style the document has generally adopted (outright spelling mistakes are ignored and only the differences are displayed).

Wait a moment and a list of spelling mistakes will appear below. Click on a word to see the context in which it appears.
