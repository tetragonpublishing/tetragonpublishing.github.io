I'm delighted to announce that Tetragon has recently been accepted as a member of the Camden Collective, a hub for the arts in Camden. Our new address will be 37 Camden High Street, London NW1 7JE. It's a short walk from home, there's a ping-pong table and it's pretty much opposite Leya's – what more could you ask for? If you'd like to drop by and say hello, get in touch. I'm hoping to expand the list of illustrators, copy-editors and proofreaders we work with in the near future.
